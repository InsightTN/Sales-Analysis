### Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Sales Performance Dashboard developed using Power BI, with Microsoft Excel used for data cleaning and preprocessing. The dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance, product trends, delivery metrics, and regional sales distribution.

The goal of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights that support strategic decision-making. Through data visualization and business intelligence techniques, the dashboard enables users to monitor revenue performance, identify top-performing products and cities, and evaluate delivery efficiency.

The dashboard consolidates multiple sales metrics into a single analytical interface, allowing stakeholders to easily interpret operational trends and patterns over time.

### Tools and Technologies
* Microsoft Excel
* Power BI
* Power Query	
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

### Data Preparation (Excel)
Before building the dashboard, the dataset was processed using Microsoft Excel to ensure data quality and consistency.

Key Data Cleaning Steps:

Handling Missing Values

* Removed or corrected incomplete records.

* Ensured important columns such as product category, city, and revenue were not null.

Data Standardization

* Standardized product category names.

* Normalized city names to avoid duplicates.

Data Formatting

* Converted date fields into a consistent format.

* Ensured numerical fields (revenue, quantity, delivery time) were properly formatted.

Duplicate Removal

* Eliminated repeated transaction entries to maintain accurate calculations.

* Column Structuring

Organized the dataset into clear fields such as:

* Product Category

* City

* Revenue

* Quantity

* Delivery Time

* Order Status

* Date

After cleaning, the dataset was exported and loaded into Power BI for analysis.

### 📊 Dashboard Features

The dashboard is designed to provide key performance indicators and business insights through several interactive visual components.
### 1️⃣ Sales Overview

This section summarizes the overall business performance through important KPIs.

Metrics Displayed

* Total Revenue: $30M

* Sales Volume: 99K

* Total Quantity Sold: 142K

* Average Revenue per Order: $256

Each KPI includes Month-over-Month (MoM) performance indicators, helping users quickly assess whether the business is improving or declining.
### 2️⃣ Top Products Analysis

This visualization highlights the best-performing product categories based on revenue.

Top Categories
| Product Category        | 	Revenue        |
|----------------|---------------|
|Computers & Accessories|	$3.04M |
|Bed, Bath & Table|	$2.58M |
|Furniture & Decor|	$2.52M|

Insight

The Computers & Accessories category generates the highest revenue, suggesting strong demand for technology-related products.

This insight can help businesses:

### 3️⃣ Monthly Sales Trend

The Monthly Trend chart visualizes sales patterns across months.

Observations

* Sales gradually increase during the first half of the year.

* A significant drop occurs around September, indicating a possible seasonal effect or operational disruption.

* Sales recover toward November, suggesting potential seasonal demand spikes.

Business Value
Understanding seasonal sales behavior helps organizations:

* Forecast demand

* Optimize production cycles

* Plan promotional campaigns
### 4️⃣ Top City Sales Distribution

This chart analyzes sales performance by geographical location.

Top Cities

* São Paulo

* Rio de Janeiro

* Belo Horizonte

* Brasília

* Curitiba

Insight

São Paulo dominates total sales, making it the primary market for the business. This suggests that:

Distribution centers should prioritize this region.

Marketing campaigns may yield high ROI in this city.
### 5️⃣ Delivery Time Analysis

The dashboard includes a time-series analysis of delivery performance.

Key Delivery Metrics

* Earliest Delivery: 00:01

* Latest Delivery: 23:59

* Peak Delivery Hour: 16:43

Operational Insight

The peak delivery hour indicates the time window when logistics operations are most active. Businesses can use this information to:

* Optimize delivery staffing

* Improve logistics planning

* Reduce delivery delays

### 6️⃣ Weekly Delivery Heatmap
A detailed time-series heatmap shows delivery activity across days of the week.

Benefits of the Heatmap

* Identifies high-traffic delivery days

* Detects logistics bottlenecks

* Helps in resource allocation

This type of analysis is critical for improving service velocity and operational efficiency.
